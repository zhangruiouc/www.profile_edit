基本信息

Hong Feng, Ph.D., professor, doctoral supervisor; secretary of Applied Computing Committee of ACM China, executive member of CCF Distributed Computing and Systems Special Committee, topic editor of Sensors, and member of the IEEE 1851 International Standards Working Group; His research interest is smart sensing and trajectory big data mining. He has presided over 2 general projects and youth projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China; and participated in more than 10 national-level research projects such as the National Key Research and Development Program, the 973 Program, and the 863 Program. He has published more than 80 academic papers in international academic journals and conferences including ACM Computing Survey, CHI, INFOCOM, etc.

招生要求

我们的团队主要面向物联网智能感知、轨迹大数据挖掘展开研究。招生要求：
1、对智能感知或轨迹大数据挖掘有强烈研究兴趣；
2、有良好的数学基础（高等数学、线性代数、概率论）；
3、具备信号分析、通信原理相关的基本知识或数据统计、机器学习、深度学习基础知识；
4、具有Android、Python或Matlab开发经验和动手能力。
*同时欢迎未来想从事科研工作的本科生加入团队。
